Practical Safety Tips

Simple actions you can take today to protect your family

Change your router password

Never use the factory default.

Disable mic & camera access

On smart TVs, speakers, and apps.

Limit in-game chat for teens

Review friend requests daily.

Beware of AI voice cloning

Always verify urgent calls in person.

Use strong unique passwords

And a password manager.

Enable two-factor authentication

On all family accounts.

Check app permissions

Before installing anything new.

Keep devices updated

Always install firmware updates.

Use a VPN on public Wi-Fi

Especially when away from home.

Talk to your children

About online safety and gaming rules.
