SAFETY TIPS

Change router password

Never leave it on factory default.

Disable mic & camera

On smart TVs and voice assistants.

Limit in-game chat

Especially for teenagers.

Beware of AI voice cloning

Always verify urgent calls in person.

Use strong unique passwords

And a password manager.

Enable two-factor authentication

On all family accounts.
