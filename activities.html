Our Activities & Roles

Router and Home Network Security

Demonstrator: JOEL MENSAH KORDIE ANKAMAH-BOATENG (Group Leader)

Live demonstration showing how default router passwords allow anyone to access the entire home network. Visitors see the admin panel being opened without any special tools.

Smart TV and Connected Devices

Demonstrator: BENEFO FRIMPONG MICHEAL

Shows what permissions smart TVs and connected devices request during setup, including microphone and camera access, and how data is shared with third parties.

Gaming Addiction and Spending Mechanics

Demonstrator: SULEMANA AKUNATU ABDUL JALIL

Explains how popular games use manipulative spending systems (in-game purchases) that can drain family mobile money accounts without parents noticing.

In-Game Stranger Danger and Grooming

Demonstrator: IBRAHIM ABDUL AZIZ

Demonstrates how strangers can contact children through in-game chat systems with almost no barriers, highlighting real grooming risks.

Safe Gaming Practices and Parental Controls

Demonstrator: YAMOAH PRINCE

Practical guide showing parents how to set up parental controls, monitor playtime, and teach children safe gaming habits.

Smart Speaker and Voice Data

Demonstrator: MOHAMMED CHIMSI

Shows what smart speakers actually record, how voice data is sent to servers, and simple ways to limit unwanted listening.

Home CCTV and Camera Privacy

Demonstrator: GUUYIRE DANIEL WENAWOME

Live demo revealing how cheap CCTV cameras can be accessed remotely if default settings are not changed, showing the privacy risks.

Voice Cloning and AI Impersonation

Demonstrator: SALAKPI CYRIL SCON

Real-time demonstration of how AI can clone a family member's voice from a short recording to create convincing scam calls.

AI Data Profiling and Phone Permissions

Demonstrator: BUKARI SAMUEL AMOAKO

Shows how apps and AI features on phones build detailed profiles of users through permissions, and how to review and limit them.

AI Scams and Fake Content

Demonstrator: FURO ISAIAH MWIGUMA

Demonstration of deepfake content and AI-generated scams, teaching families how to spot and avoid them.
