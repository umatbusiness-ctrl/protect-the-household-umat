About the Campaign

The "Protect the Household" is a student-led cybersecurity awareness campaign from the University of Mines and Technology (UMaT), Tarkwa. We aim to educate families about the hidden risks of smart home devices, online gaming, surveillance systems, and AI threats.

Our Main Objectives

Raise Awareness

Help families understand real cybersecurity threats affecting daily life.

Provide Practical Solutions

Teach simple steps to secure routers, devices, and online accounts.
