About the Campaign

The "Protect the Household" campaign is a student-led initiative from the University of Mines and Technology (UMaT), Tarkwa. Our goal is to raise awareness about cybersecurity threats affecting modern Ghanaian families, especially those using smart devices, online gaming, and AI tools.

Our Objectives

Educate Families

Teach practical ways to secure smart home devices and protect personal data.

Build Awareness

Highlight real risks like voice cloning, gaming scams, and surveillance threats.
