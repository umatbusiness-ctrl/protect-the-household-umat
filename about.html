About the Campaign

Families in Tarkwa are adopting smart devices faster than they learn the risks. Our campaign showed real dangers in routers, gaming, surveillance, and AI — and gave every family simple steps to protect themselves.

Our Objectives

  • Teach parents real smart-device risks
  • Help teenagers game safely
  • Explain what home devices actually record
  • Show live AI threats (voice cloning, scams)
  • Give every family one easy action today
  • Build a lasting public resource
